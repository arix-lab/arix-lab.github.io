AI-Native Intelligence for Physical Systems

Developing physics-aware artificial intelligence for next-generation wireless communications, computational imaging, and intelligent sensing systems.

Explore Research

Mission

ΛRIX Lab is an interdisciplinary research initiative focused on building intelligent systems at the intersection of artificial intelligence, wireless communications, computational imaging, and physical modeling. Our goal is to develop AI methods that understand, predict, and optimize complex physical systems.

Research Pillars

AI-Native Wireless Systems

6G networks, V2X communication, joint communication and sensing, digital twins, and near-field electromagnetic systems.

Computational Imaging

Quantitative phase imaging, computational microscopy, biomedical optics, and AI-based image reconstruction.

Physics-Informed AI

Machine learning approaches for inverse problems, wave propagation, and scientific discovery.

Featured Projects

Evo-WISVA

Physics-aware generative AI framework for spatio-temporal wireless channel prediction and autonomous networks.

4D Fresnel Symbol Modulation

A near-field communication framework leveraging Fresnel-domain electromagnetic properties for future wireless systems.

Computational Imaging AI

AI-enabled reconstruction methods for quantitative phase imaging and biomedical microscopy.
